About Us

Coming from a long family tradition of Italian chefs, Lorenzo was born into culinary arts. Started working at the family restaurant in Toscana at a very young age. Later he honed his cooking skills at finest Italian restaurants in Rome. Now, he has set up on a quest, to bring the best traditional Italian cuisine abroad. His path led him to Zagreb, Croatia, where he opened Lorenzo's Italian Restaurant at the very center of the Croatia's capitol.

Bringing you finest of the of the natural ingredients, Lorenzo guarantees all served dishes are cooked from organic, chemical-free vegetables. Combining tradition with contemporary requirements, Lorenzo's offer wide variety of gluten-free, vegetarian and vegan dishes tailored to all lifestyles.
